Is your father a hero?

timesofmalta.com is on the look out for Malta???s heroic dads

You don’t have to be David Beckham to be a hero to your kids.

It’s Fathers’ Day on Sunday and timesofmalta.com is making an appeal to readers who would like to nominate their father, stepfather, grandfather or even great grandfather as the best dad ever.

At times, the little heroic acts of dads seem to fade in the background but we believe that they deserve to be in the limelight as much as any celebrity. So email us today on [email protected] with your story, photos and videos clips.

The best stories will be featured in the paper on Sunday.

What better Father's Day gift is there than telling your dad he’s your hero?
