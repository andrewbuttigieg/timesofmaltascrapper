Cassola submits nomination for AD leader

Former Alternattiva Demokratika chairman Arnold Cassola has submitted his nomination for a return to the post.

Prof. Cassola submitted his nomination in the past days after the party postponed its general meeting as no nominations for party chairman were submitted.

The closing date for nominations was then postponed until June 13. Prof. Cassola’s nomination is the only one so far.

He said this afternoon he hoped his nomination would be a bridge between the next 12 months, when European Parliament elections are due, and the future, when there would hopefully be others who would offer their services to the party.

Prof. Cassola served as chairman of AD between June 2008 and October 2009
