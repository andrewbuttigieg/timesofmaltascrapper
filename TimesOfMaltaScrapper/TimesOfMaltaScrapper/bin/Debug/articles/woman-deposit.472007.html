Woman charged with partner's attempted murder ordered to pay deposit

The Criminal Court today ordered Carmen Meilak, 44, of Marsa, the woman accused of the attempted murder of her partner Alfred Farrugia, to pay a deposit of €1,000.

Ms Meilak, allegedly stabbed Mr Farrugia on Saturday evening in Marsa.

She was on Monday granted bail against a personal guarantee of €10,000.

The Attorney General appealed the bail conditions and asked for the woman’s re-arrest.

But the Criminal Court ordered that she makes a deposit of €1,000 and confirmed the rest of the bail conditions.
