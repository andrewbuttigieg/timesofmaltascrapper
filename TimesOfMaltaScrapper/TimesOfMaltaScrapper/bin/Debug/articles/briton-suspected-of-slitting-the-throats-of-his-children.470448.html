Briton suspected of slitting the throats of his children

You need Javascript and either Adobe Flash 9 or Html5 to view this video.

French police have arrested a British man, 48, on suspicion he slit the throats of his two young children. 

The two children were found in an apartment in Lyon.

The children, ages five and ten, were discovered by their mother. The couple, while divorced, were still living together and the man was reportedly not willing to move out.
