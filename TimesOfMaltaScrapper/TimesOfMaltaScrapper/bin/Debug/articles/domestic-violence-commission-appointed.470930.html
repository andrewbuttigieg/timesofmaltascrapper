Yana Micallef Stafrace to chair Domestic Violence Commission

 

 

Lawyer Yana Micallef Stafrace has been appointed chairwoman of the Domestic Violence Commission.

Rene Laiviera is deputy chairwoman and Doris Vassallo, secretary.

The other members are Oriella Fabri, Marouska Bonnici, Rita Borg Xuereb, Josef Masini Vento, Charles Cassar, Charlene Barbara, Louise Calleja and Roberta Lepre.
