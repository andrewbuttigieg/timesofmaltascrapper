Lifeguarding service at Blue Lagoon starts today

Lifeguards of the Emergency Response and Rescue Corps will commence their duties at the Blue Lagoon in Comino today.

The lifeguarding service will run on a daily basis from 9.30am to 6pm, until the end of September.

The Corps’ hydro ambulance, Lifesaver 1, will be deployed to Comino for the duration of the service.

Last year, Lifesaver 1 was used to transfer casualties from Comino to Mgarr or Cirkewwa.

The service is possible through the financial support of the Malta Tourist Authority. Support is also being provided by the Ministry for Gozo, Transport Malta, the police, the Armed Forces of Malta and the Civil Protection Department.
