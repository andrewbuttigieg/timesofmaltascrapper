Cyclist hit by car in Siggiewi

Updated - A 40-year-old cyclist from Qormi was injured this morning when he was hit by a car in Siggiewi. He suffered a dislocation, but his injuries are not life-threatening, the police said.

The cyclist was hit by an Opel Astra that was being driven by a 52-year-old woman from Siggiewi in Triq Mgr Mikiel Azzopardi at 8.30am.

The cyclist and the driver were going in different directions when the collision occurred.
